Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Overview

Gold Snipers ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our software, website, and related services.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Gold Snipers is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Your trading data, positions, or account history
  • Your MetaTrader 5 account credentials or broker details
  • Your Telegram messages or channel content
  • Your IP address or device identifiers from the Software
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond what you provide during purchase

The Software runs entirely on your local machine or VPS. Your trading data never leaves your control.

3. Data We DO Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Purchase information: Name, email address, and payment details provided to our payment processor during checkout. We do not store payment card details directly.
  • License information: License key and associated MT5 account number(s) for validation purposes.
  • Website analytics: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for the Gold Snipers website.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data under their own privacy policies:

  • Payment Processor (Stripe): Handles payment processing, license key generation, and tax compliance. Subject to their own privacy policy.
  • Tawk.to: Live chat widget on our website. May collect standard chat interaction data.
  • Google Search Console: Website indexing and search performance data.
  • GitHub: Software distribution via GitHub Releases.

5. How Your Data Is Used

  • To process your purchase and deliver your license key
  • To validate your license and enforce plan limits
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To send important updates about the Software (you may opt out of non-essential communications)
  • To improve our website and services based on anonymized analytics

6. Data Storage & Security

Purchase and license data is stored securely by our payment processor. We retain your email and license key for as long as your license is active. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via Telegram Support.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for session management and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Tawk.to may set cookies for chat session persistence.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via our website or email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.

Questions about this policy? Contact us via Telegram Support.
