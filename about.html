The Problem We Saw

Every serious trader eventually reaches the same wall. You want to copy signals from Telegram to your MT5 account, automate your strategy, and manage risk — but every tool you find has the same issues:

Outrageous Pricing

Monthly subscriptions that climb with every feature. Basic functionality locked behind $89, $149, or $299/month tiers. You're paying more for a copier than some people pay for their entire trading education.

Forced "Pro" Upgrades

Need more than one MT5 account? That's a Pro upgrade. Want stealth TP or trailing stops? Another tier. Essential features are held hostage behind paywalls that keep climbing.

Missing Features

Most copiers just copy signals. They don't filter bad setups. They don't manage partial closes. They don't protect your SL from broker manipulation. You end up building workarounds for workarounds.

Sluggish Execution

By the time some tools parse a signal and place a trade, the entry has already moved 10 pips. In Gold trading, that's the difference between profit and a drawdown.
