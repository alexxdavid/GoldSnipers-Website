Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Gold Snipers? We have answers.

Is Gold Snipers compatible with Prop Firms? +
Yes, Gold Snipers is fully optimized for Prop Firms (FTMO, FundedNext, etc.). It supports strict lot limits, maximum spread safeguards, daily loss protection overlays, and matches standard retail execution guidelines.
How does the Telegram Copier Bridge work? +
The Python bridge runs on your local PC or VPS, listening to the Telegram channels you configure. The moment a signal is posted, it parses it and writes a formatted file to the MT5 Sandbox directory. The EA reads it and places the trade in under 0.5 seconds.
Do I need a VPS to run Gold Snipers? +
While not strictly required, we highly recommend running the EA and Bridge on a Windows VPS (Virtual Private Server) for 24/7 uptime, lowest execution latency, and stable connection to Telegram.
Which trading instruments are compatible with Gold Snipers? +
While our branding highlights Gold (XAUUSD), the EA is fully compatible with Forex currency pairs (e.g. EURUSD, GBPUSD), Yen pairs (e.g. USDJPY), Metals (Silver/XAGUSD), Oil, and major Indices (e.g. US30, NAS100). The system dynamically detects the symbol's digits and scales pip calculations automatically for any asset.
