Engineered to Protect & Scale

Stop copy-pasting signals manually and getting stopped out by broker manipulation. Automate with elite retail tech across Gold, Forex, and Indices.

⚡

Real-Time Signal Processing

Ultra-fast signal response delivers Telegram alerts to the EA in under 50ms. Low-latency infrastructure ensures no opportunity is missed, even in fast-moving Gold and Forex markets.

🛡️

Stealth TP/SL Guard

Hide your Take Profit and Stop Loss levels locally on your terminal/VPS. Your broker sees only a safety-net Emergency SL far from your real target — stopping spread sweeps, stop-hunting, and target hunting. Your real TP/SL targets stay invisible until triggered.

🪜

Grid / Ladder Scaling

Don't risk everything on one entry. Gold Snipers splits trades into anchor and pending layers, averaging your entry price on pullbacks and locking in Break-Even. Anchors and layers can have their own independent Take Profit distance and shaving percentage, giving you granular control over how each tier closes.

⚙️

Automatic Entry Management

Fully automated trade lifecycle from open to close. Harvest mode secures profits by moving tiers to break-even once profitable. Pending Delete cancels unfilled limit orders at configurable TP levels. Auto Breakeven, Auto TP Shaving, and Trailing Stop Loss work together to protect gains without manual intervention.

🧮

Dynamic Risk Management

Set position sizes, stop losses, and take profits per channel. Max Drawdown Protection enforces equity floors. Risk-free mode locks trades to break-even once in profit. Break-even engines move SL to entry automatically.

📈

Equity Protection

Maximum Drawdown Protection auto-closes all positions when your equity drops below your configured threshold. Dynamic Lot Sizing scales position volume based on account balance and risk tolerance. Prop-firm safety controls enforce spread filters and lot-size caps to protect funding challenges.

🚫

Signal Guard Cutoff

Prevent late or stale entries with configurable signal age cutoffs. If a Telegram signal is older than your threshold, the EA rejects it automatically. Late Guard Pips adds an extra layer — signals that have already moved beyond a pip distance from the posted entry are skipped entirely.

📱

Telegram Admin Controls

Change settings, close trades, move stops to break-even, and request status updates directly from Telegram. Control your copier from anywhere without touching the chart or VPS.

🤖

AI Market Insights

Real-time AI analysis of gold market conditions. Get bias, confidence, risk assessment, key support/resistance levels, FVG & Order Block data, and multi-timeframe alignment — all displayed in a clean on-chart dashboard tab. Supports OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, OpenCode, and local models via Ollama or any OpenAI-compatible API provider.
