1. Initial Setup, Upgrades & Connection Guide

Before activating the EA on your charts, you must complete the one-time authorization link between your Telegram account and the trading terminal.

🚀 The First-Time Activation Wizard

When you run the Gold Snipers Bridge (GoldSnipersBridge.exe) for the very first time, it initiates an interactive, high-speed setup console:

  • License Key: A dialog will appear asking for your Gold Snipers license key (format: GS-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX). This key is provided after purchase and is required before anything else.
  • API ID & API Hash: The console will prompt you to enter your Telegram credentials (obtained from my.telegram.org).
  • *💡 Shortcut:* If you do not have custom keys, simply press [ENTER] to automatically use our secure, pre-configured developer engine.
  • Secure Authentication: Enter your phone number (with country code, e.g., +1...). The console will ask for the OTP code sent to your Telegram App, followed by your 2-Step Verification Password (if enabled).
  • Channel Subscriptions: The Bridge will print a list of your active groups/channels. Type the numbers corresponding to the channels you want to monitor (e.g., 2, 5, 9).

🔑 License Key System

Gold Snipers uses a cloud-based license key system. Here's how it works:

  • Obtaining a License: Once you purchase a license from your provider, your license key (format: GS-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) is automatically generated and displayed on the checkout success screen, allowing you to copy it and download the installer immediately.
  • Entering Your Key: On first launch, the Bridge displays a dialog asking for your license key. Enter it once — it's stored securely on your machine.
  • Changing Your Key: Click Change License Key (green button, top-right of API Settings) to enter a new key at any time.
  • Machine Binding: Your license key is bound to your computer. It cannot be copied to another machine without admin assistance.

4.5 Unified Take Profit & Stop Loss Stealth Mode

How Stealth Mode Works

When TP/SL STEALTH is active, the EA keeps your real SL and TP targets entirely in its own memory — invisible to your broker. This prevents stop-hunting and spread manipulation.

  • Virtual Targets: The true, narrow SL and TP targets are stored locally in MT5 Global Variables (GSAE_VSL_ and GSAE_VTP_), not on the broker's server.
  • Broker Sees Nothing: Orders are placed with TP = 0 and (optionally) a wide Emergency SL as a safety net.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: The EA's StealthMonitor engine checks every 200ms. When price touches your virtual TP or SL, the EA instantly closes or partially closes the position.
  • All Features Work: Trailing stop, break-even, harvest, and manual SL/TP adjustments all update the virtual targets locally — nothing is sent to the broker.

Emergency SL Safety Net

When Stealth Mode is active, you can optionally place an Emergency Stop Loss on the broker as a safety net. This protects your account if the EA loses connection (terminal crash, internet outage, power failure).

SettingDefaultEffect
Use Emergency SL (InpUseEmergencySL)false (OFF)When ON, the broker sees a wide SL beyond your virtual SL as a safety net. When OFF, the broker sees SL = 0 — fully hidden.
Emergency SL Multiplier (InpEmergencySLMultiplier)2.0How much wider the Emergency SL is compared to your real SL. A multiplier of 2.0 means the Emergency SL is placed at 2x your normal SL distance.

How the Emergency SL works:

  • Your real SL is at, say, 2450 (50 pips below entry at 2500).
  • With multiplier 2.0, the Emergency SL is placed at 2400 (100 pips below entry).
  • If the EA crashes, the broker will close the trade at 2400 — worse than your intended 2450, but prevents unlimited loss.
  • If the EA is running, it monitors the virtual SL at 2450 and closes before the Emergency SL is ever reached.

When to use Emergency SL:

  • ON — Recommended for unattended trading (VPS, overnight). Provides a safety net against EA/terminal failures.
  • OFF — Maximum stealth. The broker sees SL = 0, TP = 0. No visibility into your targets. Best when you're actively monitoring or accept the disconnect risk.

6. Harvesting & Adaptive Profit Defense

Controlled via the HARVEST ON / OFF toggle button, Harvesting is an intelligent, automated risk-defense engine designed specifically for active Grid entries.

  • Objective: Secures profits on upper trade tiers during deep market pullbacks, allowing your portfolio to recover capital incrementally.
  • How it Works: When the price dives deep enough to trigger discount limit orders, the engine steps in to protect your higher-priced entry tiers. It automatically moves the Take Profit of previous upper tiers down to their own entry prices (+1.0 pip cushion).
  • The Advantage: On any minor market bounce, your upper "underwater" tiers are instantly liquidated at 100% Break-Even, freeing up your margin while leaving your deepest discount tier active to run all the way to its full profit target!
  • Threshold Guard: Uses a mathematical safety threshold (at least 50% of your grid step distance) to ensure that minor spread noise or slippage never triggers a harvest prematurely.

🧬 High-IQ Institutional Shave Architecture

To prevent the standard industry trap of "shrinking residuals" (where EAs calculate percentages on shrinking remainders, leaving microscopic open trades indefinitely), the Gold Scalper EA implements an institutional-grade, self-correcting scaling algorithm:

  • Initial Entry Caching: The moment your ticket opens, the EA mathematically "memorizes" the Original Opening Lot Volume (e.g., 0.50 Lots) inside its persistent cache memory.
  • Cumulative Target Grid: At every TP level, the engine computes the ideal Cumulative Remaining Volume rather than isolated closed amounts. (For 0.50 lots @ 20% increments: TP1 target is 0.40 lots, TP2 target is 0.30 lots).
  • Self-Correcting Cuts: The EA calculates the exact gap between the *active live volume* and the *ideal target volume* at the next trigger. This mathematically compensates for any broker slippage, spread drift, or manual micro-adjustments, ensuring a guaranteed perfect liquidation at your final target!

18. The Copier Bridge Desktop Engine

The Telegram Copier Bridge (gs-bridge.exe) is the high-speed external engine that connects your community alerts directly to your trading terminal. It logs into your Telegram account, listens to your chosen signal channels, and transforms unstructured chat text into precise trade data delivered to the EA in under 50ms via TCP socket IPC.

When launched for the first time, the bridge initiates a secure interactive setup wizard:

2. Low-Latency Signal Delivery (Socket IPC)

In the bridge's API Settings panel, scroll to the "Low-Latency Signal Delivery" section:

  • Enable Socket IPC: When checked, the bridge establishes a high-speed local TCP socket server. The EA connects to this socket and receives signals instantly via network-byte-order IPC, bypassing disk I/O entirely.
  • Performance & Ports: Reduces signal latency from ~250ms (file polling) to under 10ms (socket connection). It runs on port 28731 by default.
  • Multi-Instance: Each MT5 profile gets its own local port automatically (e.g. instance 1 on 28731, instance 2 on 28732, etc.).
  • Backward Compatibility: If the bridge has Socket IPC disabled, the EA silently falls back to file polling at a default 250ms interval. No configuration changes required.
  • MT5 Requirement: The EA must have "Allow DLL imports" enabled in the Properties → Common tab to utilize Winsock library connections.

To help you understand exactly how your signals are copied from Telegram to MetaTrader 5, here is the lifecycle of a copied trade:

  • Signal Broadcast: A signal provider posts a message in your subscribed Telegram Channel (e.g., "BUY XAUUSD @ 2030, SL: 2020, TP1: 2035, TP2: 2040").
  • Bridge Parsing: The Copier Bridge Desktop Engine instantly intercepts the message, processes it through the Strict Anti-Noise Filters, and extracts the entry price, Stop Loss, and Take Profits.
  • Secure Delivery: The Bridge delivers the parsed signal to the EA via TCP socket IPC (~50ms latency). As a fallback, it also writes a .txt signal file inside the secure shared MetaQuotes Common directory.
  • EA Detection & Context Routing: The MT5 EA immediately receives the signal, matches the Telegram Channel ID to your preset tab (CH1 or CH2), and extracts the trade context.
  • Filters & Cutoffs Evaluation: Before execution, the EA verifies:
  • Is the signal older than 60 seconds? (If yes, discarded).
  • Does it contain any configured Trade Exclusions? (If yes, ignored).
  • Is the current market price within the Entry Cutoff range? (If yes, executed).
  • Instant Execution: The EA fires the anchor trades and pending limit grid precisely matching your channel settings (CH1 or CH2 lot size, steps, and take profits).
