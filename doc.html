1. Initial Setup, Upgrades & Connection Guide

Before activating the EA on your charts, you must complete the one-time authorization link between your Telegram account and the trading terminal.

🚀 The First-Time Activation Wizard

When you run the Gold Snipers Bridge (GoldSnipersBridge.exe) for the very first time, it initiates an interactive, high-speed setup console:

  • License Key: A dialog will appear asking for your Gold Snipers license key (format: GS-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX). This key is provided after purchase and is required before anything else.
  • API ID & API Hash: The console will prompt you to enter your Telegram credentials (obtained from my.telegram.org).
  • *💡 Shortcut:* If you do not have custom keys, simply press [ENTER] to automatically use our secure, pre-configured developer engine.
  • Secure Authentication: Enter your phone number (with country code, e.g., +1...). The console will ask for the OTP code sent to your Telegram App, followed by your 2-Step Verification Password (if enabled).
  • Channel Subscriptions: The Bridge will print a list of your active groups/channels. Type the numbers corresponding to the channels you want to monitor (e.g., 2, 5, 9).

🔑 License Key System

Gold Snipers uses a cloud-based license key system. Here's how it works:

  • Obtaining a License: Once you purchase a license from your provider, your license key (format: GS-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) is automatically generated and displayed on the checkout success screen, allowing you to copy it and download the installer immediately.
  • Entering Your Key: On first launch, the Bridge displays a dialog asking for your license key. Enter it once — it's stored securely on your machine.
  • Changing Your Key: Click Change License Key (green button, top-right of API Settings) to enter a new key at any time.
  • Machine Binding: Your license key is bound to your computer. It cannot be copied to another machine without admin assistance.

2. Dashboard Risk Management & Setup Metrics

The top-right quadrant of your Copier Dashboard features a dynamic, real-time Risk Projection Matrix. This engine displays your active sizing parameters and instantly calculates your maximum capital exposure before you fire your ladder.

  • ANCHOR LOT: The specific lot size assigned individually to each market (Anchor) order.
  • PENDING COUNT: The number of pending limit orders placed at each individual tier down the ladder grid. (If set to 2, the EA places a twin-order block at every grid step).
  • PENDING LOT: The lot size assigned individually to each pending limit order.
  • LAYER COUNT: Sets the total depth of the ladder (number of limit grid tiers) generated down the chart. A Layer Count of 5 will generate 5 distinct pending levels spaced at your configured pip-step intervals.
  • STOP LOSS (PIPS): The protective distance (in pips) for the entire grid family, measured relative to the primary anchor entry price.
  • MAX RISK CALCULATOR: A dynamic, real-time math engine projecting your worst-case total loss scenario:
  • The Assessment: The EA instantly calculates the exact dollar and percentage loss if your initial Anchors are hit, AND every single configured Pending Layer is 100% filled, before the entire family hits your Stop Loss.
  • Dynamic Color Warning: Displays in Green (Safe) for conservative exposure, scaling into Amber/Red (Aggressive) as risk percentages grow, ensuring you maintain ultimate oversight of your account margin.

4.5 Unified Take Profit & Stop Loss Stealth Mode

The Stealth TP/SL Mode (enabled via TP MODE: STEALTH or setting InpCx_TPMode = 1) allows you to hide your actual profit and loss targets from your broker to avoid stop-hunting and spread manipulation, while maintaining institutional-grade protections.

  • Broker Target Camouflage: When Stealth Mode is activated, the EA places orders with a Take Profit of 0 and a far-out Emergency Stop Loss (calculated as your configured SL multiplied by InpEmergencySLMultiplier, protected by the broker's minimum Stops Level). This ensures that your broker sees no active profit targets and only a distant emergency exit line in case of network or power failure.
  • Local Virtual Targets: The true, narrow virtual SL and TP targets are kept entirely on-chart and persisted to MT5 Global Variables (GSAE_VSL_ and GSAE_VTP_).
  • Real-Time Tick Tracking: The EA's background trading engine monitors price ticks in real-time. The moment the market Bid/Ask price touches your virtual TP or SL, the engine fires instant market execution commands to close your positions.
  • Dynamic Moves Propagation: Any automated trailing stop moves, manual "PUSH SL" adjustments, or Break-Even locks automatically update your virtual global targets (GSAE_VSL_) locally instead of sending stop modifications to the broker, automatically shifting the broker-side Emergency SL to keep it aligned as a safety net.
  • Tactile Chart Visualization: Renders premium dashed indicators directly on your chart:
  • VTP (Green Dashed Line): Indicates the current virtual Take Profit target.
  • VSL (Red Dashed Line): Indicates the current virtual Stop Loss target.
  • Clean Local Analytics: All exits are logged in your local SQLite Analytics database with clear exit comments (e.g. "STEALTH_TP" or "STEALTH_SL"), while broker-side history comments remain completely generic.

6. Harvesting & Adaptive Profit Defense

Controlled via the HARVEST ON / OFF toggle button, Harvesting is an intelligent, automated risk-defense engine designed specifically for active Ladder grids.

  • Objective: Secures profits on upper trade tiers during deep market pullbacks, allowing your portfolio to recover capital incrementally.
  • How it Works: When the price dives deep enough to trigger discount limit orders, the engine steps in to protect your higher-priced entry tiers. It automatically moves the Take Profit of previous upper tiers down to their own entry prices (+1.0 pip cushion).
  • The Advantage: On any minor market bounce, your upper "underwater" tiers are instantly liquidated at 100% Break-Even, freeing up your margin while leaving your deepest discount tier active to run all the way to its full profit target!
  • Threshold Guard: Uses a mathematical safety threshold (at least 50% of your grid step distance) to ensure that minor spread noise or slippage never triggers a harvest prematurely.

🧬 High-IQ Institutional Shave Architecture

To prevent the standard industry trap of "shrinking residuals" (where EAs calculate percentages on shrinking remainders, leaving microscopic open trades indefinitely), the Gold Scalper EA implements an institutional-grade, self-correcting scaling algorithm:

  • Initial Entry Caching: The moment your ticket opens, the EA mathematically "memorizes" the Original Opening Lot Volume (e.g., 0.50 Lots) inside its persistent cache memory.
  • Cumulative Target Grid: At every TP level, the engine computes the ideal Cumulative Remaining Volume rather than isolated closed amounts. (For 0.50 lots @ 20% increments: TP1 target is 0.40 lots, TP2 target is 0.30 lots).
  • Self-Correcting Cuts: The EA calculates the exact gap between the *active live volume* and the *ideal target volume* at the next trigger. This mathematically compensates for any broker slippage, spread drift, or manual micro-adjustments, ensuring a guaranteed perfect liquidation at your final target!

When launched for the first time, the bridge initiates a secure interactive setup wizard:

2. Low-Latency Signal Delivery (Socket IPC)

In the bridge's API Settings panel, scroll to the "Low-Latency Signal Delivery" section:

  • Enable Socket IPC: When checked, the bridge establishes a high-speed local TCP socket server. The EA connects to this socket and receives signals instantly via network-byte-order IPC, bypassing disk I/O entirely.
  • Performance & Ports: Reduces signal latency from ~250ms (file polling) to under 10ms (socket connection). It runs on port 28731 by default.
  • Multi-Instance: Each MT5 profile gets its own local port automatically (e.g. instance 1 on 28731, instance 2 on 28732, etc.).
  • Backward Compatibility: If the bridge has Socket IPC disabled, the EA silently falls back to file polling at a default 250ms interval. No configuration changes required.
  • MT5 Requirement: The EA must have "Allow DLL imports" enabled in the Properties → Common tab to utilize Winsock library connections.

2. Intelligent Parsing & Conversational Commands

The bridge's text interpreter is designed to extract precise trading data from diverse, unstructured formats:

20. Advanced Guide: Trade Exclusions, Command Routing & Copier Workflow

This section provides an advanced, user-friendly breakdown of how the Copier Bridge and the Gold Scalper EA interact under the hood, and how to surgically customize trade exclusions, keywords, and automated remote commands.

🔄 The End-to-End Copier Workflow

To help you understand exactly how your signals are copied from Telegram to MetaTrader 5, here is the lifecycle of a copied trade:

  • Signal Broadcast: A signal provider posts a message in your subscribed Telegram Channel (e.g., "BUY XAUUSD @ 2030, SL: 2020, TP1: 2035, TP2: 2040").
  • Bridge Parsing: The Copier Bridge Desktop Engine instantly intercepts the message, processes it through the Strict Anti-Noise Filters, and extracts the entry price, Stop Loss, and Take Profits.
  • Secure Delivery: The Bridge delivers the parsed signal to the EA via named pipe IPC (~50ms latency). As a fallback, it also writes a .tmp file that swaps to a finalized .txt signal file inside the secure shared MetaQuotes Common directory.
  • EA Detection & Context Routing: The MT5 EA immediately receives the signal, matches the Telegram Channel ID to your preset tab (CH1 or CH2), and extracts the trade context.
  • Filters & Cutoffs Evaluation: Before execution, the EA verifies:
  • Is the signal older than 60 seconds? (If yes, discarded).
  • Does it contain any configured Trade Exclusions? (If yes, ignored).
  • Is the current market price within the Entry Cutoff range? (If yes, executed).
  • Instant Execution: The EA fires the anchor trades and pending limit grid precisely matching your channel settings (CH1 or CH2 lot size, steps, and take profits).

🚫 How to Configure Trade Exclusions (Keyword Blocking)

You can configure Trade Exclusions to prevent specific setups from firing. This is highly useful if a channel provider posts a setup you want to ignore, or posts standard chat messages that look like trades.

  • Exclusion Keywords Box: Located in your Copier UI under the Parsing Settings tab.
  • How to Add Exclusions: Enter comma-separated keywords into the Exclusions text box (e.g. pending, test, limit, break, demo).
  • Surgical Filtering: If an incoming message contains any of your configured exclusion keywords, the Bridge instantly blocks the signal. The EA will not open any trades, and the Copier log footer will record: "Signal skipped due to exclusion keyword filter."

💻 Automated Remote Commands (Conversational Triggers)

The Copier Bridge does not just fire new setups; it listens for active updates from channel leaders to automatically protect your open positions:

  • Breakeven Commands (CMD,RISK_FREE):
  • Triggers: Channel updates containing terms like risk free, secure profit, move sl to be, break even, lock profits, sl to entry, or secure now.
  • Action: The Copier Bridge translates this into a high-speed command. The MT5 EA immediately moves the Stop Loss of all active trades in that channel's family to their entry price plus a safety spread buffer, instantly locking in safety.
  • Close All Commands (CMD,CLOSE_ALL):
  • Triggers: Channel updates containing terms like close all, close now, exit trade, cancel setup, close gold, i am out, or exit now.
  • Action: The Copier Bridge immediately signals a total flat command. The MT5 EA forcefully liquidates all open positions and deletes all pending limit grid levels for that specific channel's family, securing your cash profits immediately.
